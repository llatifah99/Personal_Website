Final Project: Attendance Machine With Covid-19 Protocols Management Using Raspberry Pi

Research Background

School closures due to COVID-19 have some negative impacts on students, such as declining student abilities, Higher Dropout Rates, and disrupting emotional development and psychological health. To prevent learning loss during the pandemic, the government allows schools to carry out face-to-face learning by implementing the Covid-19 protocol.

Research Question

How to build an integrated framework for attendance with Covid-19 protocols management. The protocols include: checking body temperature, mask, and automatic hand sanitizer

Scope and Limitation

  1. Presence recording using RFID
  2. Checking the use of masks is detected at a certain distance.
  3. The student's face must face forward.
  4. The student's face will be detected in the provided frame.

Purpose

To Create an attendance machine that can check temperature, mask, and provide automatic hand sanitizer. So, it can simplify the task of Satgas Covid-19 in screening students before entering the school area.

The system works

Students tap their cards on the RFID reader. If the student card is registered, the temperature sensor and the camera will turn on. there are 3 conditions: When the temperature sensor reads more than 37.5 degrees, Student data will be recorded in the database. alarm will go off, and data send via telegram. When the temperature is normal and wearing a mask, the data will be stored in the database. The Hand Sanitizer will come out automatically because the servo is moving. When the student is not wearing a mask or the student card is not registered, the alarm will go off
