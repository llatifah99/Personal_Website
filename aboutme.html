About me


I am Lita an electrical engineering enthusiast with a keen interest in web development. My academic background in electrical engineering has equipped me with a foundation in problem-solving and analytical skills. However, my passion for the dynamic world of web development has led me to expand my skill set and explore the ever-evolving landscape of programming languages and frameworks.

Driven by the desire to bridge the gap between hardware and software, I find joy in creating interactive and user-friendly web applications. The process of transforming ideas into functional and visually appealing websites fascinates me, and I am committed to continuous learning and staying abreast of the latest trends in the tech industry. As I navigate the intersection of electrical engineering and web development, I am excited about contributing to innovative projects that bring about positive change in the digital realm.
